## Research Papers (Zenodo)

### Excess Vocabulary in Japanese AI-Generated Text: A Cross-Model Quantitative Analysis
- DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.19233934
- Date: March 2026
- Topics: Excess Vocabulary, Japanese NLP, LLM Detection, MeCab, Cross-Model Comparison, Vocabulary Coevolution
- Dataset: 350 AI samples (7 models) vs. 977 human articles
- Key finding: 651 statistically significant excess words; cross-domain classifier AUC=0.946 but fails across genres
- Code & Data: https://github.com/kenimo49/excess-vocabulary-ja

### AI Text Slop: A Quantitative Study of Stylistic Convergence Across Six Language Models in Japanese Technical Writing
- DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.19173035
- Date: March 2026
- Topics: LLM, AI Text Detection, Japanese NLP, Stylistic Convergence, RLHF, AI Slop
- Dataset: 180 samples (6 models × 10 topics × 3 trials)
- Key finding: RLHF models score higher on slop metrics (Cohen's d = 1.01); Swallow Paradox
- Code & Data: https://github.com/kenimo49/ai-text-slop

### AI Blue: Systematic Color Recognition Bias in Vision-Language Models
- DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.19159702
- Date: March 2026
- Topics: Vision-Language Models, Color Perception, CIEDE2000, AI Slop, Computer Vision
- Dataset: 4 VLMs × 40 colors × 480 observations
- Key finding: 95% of AI-generated UI colors land on blue-purple
- Code & Data: https://github.com/kenimo49/ai-blue-color-bias
