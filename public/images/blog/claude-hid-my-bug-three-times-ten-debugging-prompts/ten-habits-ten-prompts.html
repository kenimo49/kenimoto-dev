10 Debugging Habits → 10 Prompt Fragments
What to paste into the prompt or into CLAUDE.md so the model investigates instead of suppresses.
1
Doubt the inputs
Confirm logs are complete before diagnosing
2
Reproduce first
If you can't reproduce, say so and stop
3
Find the boundary
Where does correct turn into broken?
4
Diff vs known-good
Run git log --oneline -20 and look
5
Build a timeline
Sudden or gradual? Tie to deploys
6
Audit retry/cache/timeout
What if the call is slow but not failed?
7
Spot amplification
Small error multiplied into a storm?
8
Instrument, don't guess
Propose logs to add, not a fake fix
9
Simplify the suspect
Smallest input that still triggers it
10
Break on purpose
Predict the outcome before running
