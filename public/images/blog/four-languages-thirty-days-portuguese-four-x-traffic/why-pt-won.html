Why PT pulled ahead
three asymmetries, one outcome
1
A real community door
TabNews is a Brazilian developer hub with no English equivalent. Posting a translated article there gets read; posting the same in EN goes nowhere unless you already have an audience.
referral asymmetry
2
Thinner AI-search competition
English LLMO content is a saturated market. Portuguese AI-search SERPs have fewer technical blogs fighting for the same prompts, so the first decent answer wins.
SOV asymmetry
3
Early-mover on llms.txt
Major BR dev sites do not ship /pt/llms.txt yet. Being one of the few sites that does feeds AI crawlers a clean menu in their language.
crawler asymmetry
